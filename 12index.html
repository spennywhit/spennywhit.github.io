JNL 221: Data Visualization Assignment 3

Trucks and personal vehicles are the two most popular methods for traveling from Canada to the U.S, with both crossing about 11 million times each since the beginning of 2020. Loaded truck containers sit in third place with roughly 8.6 million crossings to the U.S from Canada since January of 2020. Ranked fourth, around 3.5 million loaded rail containers have traveled to the U.S from Canada since January of 2020. In the same timespan, about 2.7 million empty truck containers have traveled to the U.S from Canada. And 1.5 million empty rail containers have made the same trip in the same time window. Pedestrians, trains, and buses were the least popular method of traveling from Canada to the U.S, with each method having roughly 87,000, 42,000, and 14,000 crossings each since January of 2020, respectively.
